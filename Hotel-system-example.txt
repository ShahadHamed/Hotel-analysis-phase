5. COMPLETE HOTEL SYSTEM EXAMPLE
US-001: Secure Guest Login

User Story:
As a guest, I want to log in securely to the hotel portal, so that I can view my bookings and make payments safely.

Acceptance Criteria:

Given I am a registered guest, when I enter correct credentials and click “Login,” I am redirected to my dashboard.

When credentials are incorrect, display “Invalid credentials” and lock account after 5 failed attempts.

Password reset link expires after 24 hours.

Sessions expire after 15 minutes of inactivity.

All login attempts are logged for security.

Story Points: 8
